In 2023, we reinforced Vestiaire Collective’s position as the leading destination for pre-loved fashion by collaborating with top stylists like Erin Walsh and Marylin Fitoussi. We developed The Stylist Collective, a series of masterclasses offering expert insights on vintage finds, curated edits, and styling inspiration. Hosted by Natacha Morice, the series brought a fresh perspective to sustainable fashion.
